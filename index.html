About Staten Island Therapeutic Gardens

Staten Island Therapeutic Gardens is dedicated to advancing food justice and fostering healthy communities through the power of gardening. During the pandemic, the organization played a pivotal role in ensuring that Staten Islanders in need received safe, no-contact deliveries of fresh food. In addition to direct assistance, they hosted Pop-Up Produce events across the North Shore, distributing free, healthy produce to hundreds of families.

Through partnerships with local organizations, we provide essential nourishment and empower the community with resources to access food banks and connect with elected officials to address their needs.

Staten Island
Therapeutic Gardens

Staten Island Therapeutic Gardens is dedicated to advancing food justice and fostering healthy communities through the power of gardening.

  • Revitalization

    Through hands-on cleanup initiatives and collaborative maintenance efforts, we rejuvenate local parks and natural areas, creating sanctuaries where residents can connect with nature and each other.

  • Nourishment

    In addition to direct assistance, we've hosted Pop-Up Produce events across the North Shore, distributing free, healthy produce to hundreds of families.

  • Resilience

    During the pandemic, our organization played a pivotal role in ensuring that Staten Islanders in need received safe, no-contact deliveries of fresh food.

  • Empowerment

    Through partnerships with local organizations, we provide essential nourishment and empower the community with resources to access food banks and connect with elected officials to address their needs.
